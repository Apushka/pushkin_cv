Makeup brand
fullstack application

  • NodeJS, Express backend

    Server provides:

    • interaction with Firebase database
    • authorization of users
    • creation of product cards, cards content filling (photo, video, text)
    • generation of unique QR-codes for each product card

  • React frontend

    Client allows:

    • to navigate to product card after scanning QR-code via phone, tablet, etc. (QR-codes are available at company information stands)
    • browse a card without reloading
    • log in to administrator account for creation, editing and removing cards from database
    • gather statistics via Yandex.Metrika

  • Application deployment on company hosting
